What is Phloem

Phloem is a job searching tool that caters towards your unique learning ability. After inputting your skills and dream job, we will find the skills that are missing and provide you educational resources that will help fill in the gap. We especially hope to help those who may have lost their careers to automation or the COVID pandemic, including industries such as the food industry, transportation, manufacturing and hospitality.

We are also committed to supporting good in the world. That's why all the companies and job opportunities in our database have no ties to any form of oppressive governments, racism, sexism, religious intolerance, and homphobia.
